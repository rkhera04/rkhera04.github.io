About Us

I have experiences living in Korea, the United States, Singapore, and Japan. These diverse experiences have enriched my understanding of various cultures and have shaped who I am today. Currently, I am studying at Tokyo Woman's Christian University in Japan, where I am continuing to expand my knowledge and skills.

Photography is one of my greatest passions. I love capturing moments and telling stories through my lens. Whether it's with my smartphone, digital camera, film camera, or Polaroid camera, I enjoy experimenting with different formats and styles. Each photograph allows me to express my creativity and connect with others on a deeper level.

As I continue my journey, I am excited to share my experiences and insights with you through this website. I hope to inspire others to see the world through a different lens and to appreciate the small, beautiful moments that make life extraordinary.
